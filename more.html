IPv6 Monitoring

The data gathered by the monitoring client is displayed in a number of figures as described below. Additional details are provided in the page associated with each figure. Figures can be scaled by selecting a corresponding area in the figure, and additional information can be obtained about individual data points by positioning the mouse cursor on top of them.

  • Fig. 1: Plots the percentage of IPv6 reachable sites among all the sites queried by the monitoring client.
  • Fig. 1.2: Plots the percentage of IPv6 reachable sites among the top 1M sites based on Alexa's ranking.
  • Fig. 2: Identifies among sites that could be reached over both IPv4 and IPv6, how many offer the same, similar, or different content. The notions of same, similar, and different content are based on page sizes.
  • Fig. 3: Percentage of IPv6 reachable sites by rank.
  • Fig. 4: Scatter plot of IPv4 vs. IPv6 download times for all reachable sites.
  • Fig. 4.1: Percentage by rank of sites with a lower content download time over IPv6 than over IPv4.
  • Fig. 4.2: Difference by rank between IPv4 and IPv6 download times.
  • Fig. 5: Scatter plot of normalized (by content size) IPv4 vs. IPv6 download times for all reachable sites.
  • Fig. 5.1: Average difference between IPv4 and IPv6 download speeds by rank.
  • Fig. 6: Evolution over time of IPv6 routing table size (number of prefixes).
